ABOUT

Founded in 2021 by a team of young entrepreneurs Chic Kebab was born when the 5 founders met at an event linking them. The idea of revisiting the famous kebab using quality and refined ingredients from organic farming, directly convinced them to innovate and bring this typical dish to the level of gastronomic dishes. Already starred since then, the restaurant has opened its doors in a small town in Charleroi and plans to open soon in the cities of Paris, Milan and Dubai.
